# 01. Executive Project Overview

## 1.1 Business Objectives
**Fuwa.NG** is an enterprise-grade digital platform designed to provide unified access to identity verification, digital services, and legal workflows in Nigeria. The primary goal is to automate and streamline fragmented manual processes for businesses and individuals.

Key objectives include:
*   **Centralization**: Providing a single gateway for multiple identity verification types (NIN, BVN, CAC, etc.).
*   **Automation**: Replacing manual verification with API-driven real-time responses.
*   **Legal Tech Innovation**: Enabling AI-powered legal drafting and notary services.
*   **High Availability**: Ensuring 99.9% uptime for critical services via smart routing and failover logic.

## 1.2 Core Value Proposition
- **Unified Identity Gateway**: Access to 10+ verification types through a single API/UI.
- **Smart Routing Technology**: Automatically switches between providers (Robosttech, Dataverify, etc.) to ensure high success rates.
- **AI-Powered Legal Tech**: Generative AI (Google Gemini) integration for rapid drafting of legally binding documents.
- **Agency-Ready**: A robust wallet system with commission structures for agents and resellers.

## 1.3 Scope of Work
The project is divided into three major suites:
1.  **Identity & Verification Suite**: Connecting to national databases (NIN, BVN, FRSC, CAC, TIN).
2.  **Fuwa.NG Ecosystem**: Value-added services including AI Legal Hub, Virtual Dollar Cards, VTU (Airtime/Data), and Education services (WAEC/JAMB).
3.  **Technical & CMS Suite**: A comprehensive admin dashboard for managing API keys, dynamic pricing, audit logs, and system health.

## 1.4 Success Metrics
Success is measured by:
*   **Uptime**: 99.9% service availability.
*   **Verification Speed**: Average response time under 5 seconds for core verification services.
*   **User Retention**: Active agent/reseller growth.
*   **Auditability**: 100% logging of all verification requests and financial transactions.
*   **Scalability**: Ability to handle 10,000+ daily verification requests across all providers.
